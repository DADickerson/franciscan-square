Ice Skate This Winter at Franciscan Square

Mid-November Through February

The Fun Awaits

Outdoor Ice Skating For All Ages

Experience Stuebenville’s only outdoor ice skating rink this winter season. The Rink at Franciscan Square is open from November through February for public ice skating, special events, and more. Whether you’re a seasoned skater or stepping onto the ice for the first time, there’s something for everyone to enjoy at the Rink!

Magical Outdoor Experiences

Skate under twinkling lights in a festive winter wonderland. Toast marshmallows over an open fire while cozying up with a cup of hot cocoa.

Convenient
Location

Located at Franciscan Square in Steubenville, OH, where you can also find the Advent Market and the famous Nutcracker Village - featured on the TODAY show.

Dining
On-Site

Just steps away from the Rink, you can enjoy a warm meal at Rubi's, Jimmy Johns, or the the Harp and Habit with your family.

Skate Rentals for The Small And Tall

Rent skates on-site for all ages and sizes. Skates are sharpened and sanitized for your safety.

Skate Rentals are available for $5 per session (available up to a size 13!) making it easy for everyone to join the fun.

Skate helper rentals are also available for those wanting a little extra balance when gliding around.

Skate Sharpening Services

Own ice skates but need a tune-up? Keep your blades sharp with our professional ice skate sharpening. Drop them off when you arrive, and we’ll have them ready before you hit the ice. Perfect for the casual skater or pond hockey players alike.

Plan Ahead

Plan ahead and reserve your ice skate sharpening service to ensure availability.

Turn The Everyday Into a Holiday

Special Events

We offer more than just public skating at the Rink. Enjoy themed ice skating nights, holiday celebrations, and special events for all ages.

Group Skating

Planning a group outing, birthday, or special event? Contact us for custom group skating packages.

Play Pond Hockey on the Rink at Franciscan Square

Quick Games for All Skill Levels

Pond hockey is a fun way to enjoy the ice, whether you’re new to hockey or a seasoned vet, pond hockey is fast-paced and exciting for all!

What makes pond hockey different? We play without goalies, smaller goals, and sponge pucks for safety. We host pickup-style games for kids, students, and adults - no experience necessary!
